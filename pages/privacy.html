Privacy

Page last updated: 8/5/23

At Postr, we respect and protect your privacy. We only collect your name and email when you sign up for our platform, and we do not share any of the content you post on our platform with anyone. We do not have access to your content, and we do not have any authority over it.



1. Information We Collect

We only collect your name and email when you sign up for our platform. We do not collect any other personal information, and we do not use cookies or other tracking technologies.



2. How We Use Your Information

We use your name and email to provide you with access to our platform and to communicate with you about our services.



How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with anyone, and we do not have access to any of the content you post on our platform.



3. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all of your content will be permanently deleted from our platform.



At Postr, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe and secure platform for you to share your content.
